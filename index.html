About Us

Our Story

Elements Construction Company, formed in 2009 and later transformed into Elements Interiors, is specialized in fine finishing works, electrical works, and mechanical works. Elements applies its intensive know-how through its well reputed stakeholders and alliances, and continuously seeks fresh and innovative approaches to project management and rewarding deliverables.

Aiming for world class services, our goal is to exceed the expectations of every client by offering unparalleled project management techniques and excellent transparency, thus optimizing our clients' value for money. Our associates' solid functional and technical expertise ensures that our clients receive the most effective and professional service. Elements Interiors employs some of the finest personnel covering Labor force, Vocational specialists, Supervisors, and Project Managers. Elements Interiors has access to some of the finest freelance skilled labor force available.

Vision

While we aim to be a part of the development and growth of our region's economy, we strive to reach international standards in project management and to meet our clients' highest expectations.

Mission

  • To provide our clients with the utmost transparency through our projects' different phases;
  • To provide our clients with the best advice;
  • To provide our clients with the best attainable quality of work.

Values

  • Strong relations with our clients, suppliers, work force, associations, and ministries;
  • Trust and transparency;
  • Goals realization;
  • Respond to new market trends and needs.

Personalized Work Approach

Due to the fact that our sector involves high level of human error, hence as business owners we believe that our personal touch is integral to achieve the standards we seek to reach in every project. Therefore, our personal day-to-day interference to the slightest detail helps us reduce the margins of error to a very admirable level in comparison to much bigger volume-oriented companies in the market.

Social Responsibility and Safety Measures

As a part of our social responsibility, we always look for environmentally better ways to execute our projects. We always make sure to minimize our waste through calculated procurement procedures and we always try to promote more efficient, environmentally friendlier alternatives to our clients.

As well, we are always up-to-date with all safety procedures and safety equipment to ensure safe execution and staff wellbeing throughout our projects different phases.
